Research Interests

I work on computer networks and systems. Currently, I focus on using mathematical modeling as well as building systems to explore and understand the design of large-scale computer systems. Welcome to visit us!

Current Projects

  • Future network protocol: congestion control (Reno, Vegas, Cubic, BBR), multipath TCP, WebRTC, QUIC and HTTP2

  • Molecular communications: molecular computing and communication in biological, chemical, or mechanical environments

  • Network infrastructure/paradigm: CDN and anycast, SDN and programmable data plane (P4), SmartNIC, FPGA-based NIC

  • Distributed deep learning system: Gradient compression and in-network parameter aggregation

  • Learning and online optimization: Large-scale robust optimiaztion, online bipartite matching for ride hailing system
