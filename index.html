Research Interests

I work on computer networks and systems. Currently, I focus on using mathematical modeling as well as building systems to explore and understand the design of large-scale computer systems. Welcome to visit us!

Current Projects

  • SDN/NFV: consistent network update, resource allocation, service chain deployment

  • Data center transport: load balancing, congestion control, scheduling
