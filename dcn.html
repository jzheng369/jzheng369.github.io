Data center networking

Data center networks are a critical part of the infrastrcture in delivering performance for applications, many of which partition data and computation across a networked cluster of machines. The network fabric is shared in nature, however, and leads to highly variable latency, throughput, etc., not to mention faults and errors that hamper applications of all kinds.

We seek to design, implement, and evaluate novel solutions for various problems in data center networks. Currently, we focus on providing low latency, and dealing with failures.
